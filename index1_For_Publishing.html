Cord Diagram showing Frequency of Trips among Wards







The chart represents travel patterns between eight different wards. Each number in the chart shows how many trips people made between two specific wards. Thicker lines mean more trips were made between those wards.

For example, the number in the intersection of "Ward 6" and "Ward 2" tells us how many trips were made from Ward 6 to Ward 2. This chart helps us understand how people move around these wards.
